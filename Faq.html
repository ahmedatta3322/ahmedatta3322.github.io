Diving FAQ

 Where is your office?

We are an online based agency have no office, however we still have a location in Assalah area – Dahab where we live & also share the big space with all of our guest; meeting, greeting, operating, Jamming, hosting travelers & more. So please ask us how to reach our location when in Dahab so we can give out the location.

 What makes you different from other tour operators in Dahab or Sinai? 

We love what we do beside we are in deep love with Sinai with all what it has to offer, that`s why we would offer you a service that for sure wouldn’t find anywhere else & places to experience through with young energetic eyes that wouldn’t be full of regular tourists as we only like to serve travelers & explorers, besides we are safety concerned & count for every step ahead.

 Which part of Sinai does Sinai Gate offer the programs?

We operates around the whole South of Sinai, So we are able to offer special programs in Taba, Nuweiba, Ras Shaitan, Dahab, Sharm El-Sheikh, St. Catherine.  

 - I`m afraid of diving & being underwater, is it good to dive? 

Simply we all are scared from anything we don’t know so much about, so lack of knowledge is our worst enemy. That is why we always make sure to offer you the needed information for your diving experience, starting from the physical, physiological & psychological effects to Equipment & how to use it properly to the ocean & marine life.

 I never had a SCUBA course, can I still dive?

Sure thing, Since we are PADI certified instructors & dive masters then we can happily offer you the PADI discover SCUBA diving program, that doesn’t require any previous experience, all what it takes is 2 dives in 3 hours period to give you a peek on the underwater world in a safe & funny way.

 Why take my SCUBA courses with Sinai Gate?

Because SCUBA diving is passion to us, its somehow why some of our team members are living; to dive. So we will make our best to pass all of our knowledge, expertise & teaching techniques with you so that we can prepare a good diver to dive the world & discover as much from the oceans, beside our courses & diving education programs are not just a card you receive by completing a 3 or 4 days course, however we will be very honest with you if you are not meeting the passing requirements assigned by PADI.

Hiking FAQ

 What is hiking? 

Hiking originally means walking, but now a days it is more about walking in nature. So Hiking is being an individual human who walks through nature & the reasons can diverse from challenging yourself, break the routine, raise up the levels of your personal fitness, meditate, unite with your inner self & nature around you to plenty more of reasons.

 - Why hike with Sinai Gate & what is the safety rules you are following? 

Sinai Gate`s team are Hiking & nature lovers, We have accomplished more than 130 organized successful hikes since 2015 through different trails in South Sinai & we don’t consider the commercial half day trips hiking but as a fact it’s just trip. We always offers 2 guides for the long hikes (front local guide from the area we are hiking & back guide from our team who is emergency 1st responder with a descent mountain experience) beside that we are always training our selves & the new team joiners before they guide a group.

  Where do you Hike?

Since we operate around the whole South Sinai so we can offer you special trails in Dahab, Nuweiba, St. Catherine & around.
